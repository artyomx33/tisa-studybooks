## ğŸ“ Teacher Notes

**Discussion Prompts:**
1. "What's something your family makes at home?"
2. "What's the difference between making something and buying it?"
3. "Why do factories use machines instead of making everything by hand?"

**Real-World Connections:**
- Cooking at home (production process)
- Factories they've seen or visited
- Where their toys/clothes come from
- Recycling (renewable resources)

**Common Misconceptions:**
- All goods are things you can touch (intangible goods exist too!)
- Factories only make big things (they make small items too)
- Resources never run out (non-renewable resources do!)
