## üìù Teacher Notes

**Discussion Prompts:**
1. "What happens when everyone tries to do everything?"
2. "What are YOU best at? What's your specialisation?"
3. "Why do doctors focus on medicine instead of also building houses?"

**Real-World Connections:**
- School staff (teachers teach, cooks cook, cleaners clean)
- Family roles (who cooks? who cleans? who drives?)
- Factory assembly lines

**Key Concepts to Emphasise:**
- **Division of Labour** = Splitting work so each person does their best task
- **Specialisation** = Becoming really good at ONE thing
- **Efficiency** = Getting more done in less time when specialised
- **Teamwork** = Everyone contributes their special skill

**Classic Economics Connection:**
This lesson teaches Adam Smith's famous "pin factory" concept ‚Äî when workers specialise (one person draws wire, one cuts, one sharpens), they produce FAR more than if each person made entire pins alone!
